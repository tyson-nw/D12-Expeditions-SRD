# Our Goals

The goal is to create a broad system that is simple to learn and consistent in it mechanical system so if you know one system, you are already familiar when presented with a new system. We are building a system that supports tactical combat but with robust roleplaying and non-combat support. It is pretty obvious that this is a derivation of various D20 systems, but we think our distillation is unique in two ways.

## Encourages teamwork

From out combat design to complex rolls we want players to be collaborating at every turn. They are encouraged to use their actions to set up their allies to go above and beyond what they can do on their own.

## Encourages Engagement

Too often as soon as a character no longer has the spotlight the player will step away and disengage to some extent. We hope that working together to overcome conflicts and providing reactive features that can allow characters to intervene on their allies behalf will encourage engagement.
