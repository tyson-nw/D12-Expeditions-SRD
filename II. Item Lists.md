# Alchemical Items
## Tier 1

###### Antitoxin
Drinking the Antitoxin grants the drinker an Edge on Body Defenses against ongoing poison effects and the poison's Potency reduces by 4 per save.

###### Bolstering Draught

Drinking the Bolstering Draught grants 2d8+2 [Temporary HP](app://obsidian.md/Temporary%20HP) .

###### Fire bomb

Targets within Circle 1 take 2d6 damage. As a Perk the targets is Burning.

###### Poison

When applied to a Bladed or Piercing weapon poison deals an 1d8 damage the first time that weapon hits after application. As a Maneuver the Poison can be applied to a weapon. As a Perk the target is Sickened until the Potency of the item drops below their Body Defense.

###### Pepper Bomb

Targets within Circle 1 are Exposed. As a Perk the target loses [Response](app://obsidian.md/Response)s until the end of their turn.

###### Smoke Bomb

A Circle 2 fills with smoke, obscuring and blinding all targets within the smoke until the beginning of the next turn.

###### Tar Bomb

Targets within Circle 1 are Grabbed until their Body defense overcomes the Potency. As a Perk they are Restrained.

###### Thunderstone

Targets within Circle 3 are Deafened until the end of their next turn.

## Tier 2

###### Anointed Oil

When applied to a weapon, Anointed oil deals an additional 2d8 damage to Aberrations, Fiends, or Undead.

###### Blink Bomb

A Circle 0 area is filled with smoke, the creature within the area teleports anywhere they can see within 12 spaces.

###### Curative Draught

Drinking the Curative draught recovers 1d8+1 hitpoints.

###### Dragon Draught

Drinking the Dragon Draught gives the drinker a dragon's breath weapon. As an Action the drinker can deal 2d8 damage to targets within a 3 space Cone. The breath weapon is available until the Potency of the draught reduces to 0.

###### Flare Bomb

Targets within Circle 2 are Blinded and Deafened. At the end the target's turn the potency of the Tar Bomb reduces by 2 until the target's Body defense overcomes it. As a Perk they are incapacitated until the end of their Turn.

###### Invigorating Draught

Drinking the Invigorating Draught grants an Edge on the drinker's next Roll.

###### Might Draught

Drinking the Might Draught adds 1d6 to the drinker's Strength Rolls until the Potency of the Draught reduces to 6.

###### Recovering Draught

Drinking the Recovering Draught reduces the drinker's Blight by 1.

###### Tacky Fire bomb

Targets within Circle 1 are on fire and take 2d6 damage at the end of their Turn that cannot be snuffed until the target's React Defense overcomes the Potency. As a Perk the targets is Blinded until the the fire is snuffed.

###### Tough Hide Draught

Drinking the Tough Hide Draught sets the drinker's Soak 4.

## Tier 3

###### Elemental Infusion

When applied to a weapon, the weapon deals an additional 2d8 damage until the Potency reduces below 14.

###### Invisibility Draught

Drinking the Invisibility Draught renders the drinker Invisible until the Potency of reduces below 14.

###### Spell Bomb

A Tier 1 spell the crafter can cast can be bound to the Spell Bomb. It has a Thrown 6 tag and the spell is centered on the space where the Spell Bomb is triggered or effects the target that is hit by the Spell Bomb.

###### Stone Draught

Drinking the Stone Draught sets the drinker's Soak to 8.

## Tier 4

###### Improved Spell Bomb

A Tier 2 spell the crafter can cast can be bound to the Spell Bomb. It has a Thrown 6 tag and the spell is centered on the space where the Spell Bomb is triggered or effects the target that is hit by the Spell Bomb.

###### Diving Balestra

_Legacy, Weapon, [Piercing](app://obsidian.md/SRD/Glossary/Piercing.md)_

The base item is a Rapier.

1. **Cavalier Leap** - The wielder can move their speed in a straight line in any direction as a [Maneuver](app://obsidian.md/Maneuver).
2. **Gallant Charge** - As a [Maneuver](app://obsidian.md/Maneuver) the the wielder can move their speed in a straight line, moving through enemy spaces without treating them as [Difficult Terrain](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Difficult%20Terrain) or provoke [Movement Responses](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Movement%20Responses).
3. **Diving Flurry** - When making a Galant Charge the wielder can make a [Strength](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Strength)+[Weapon](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Weapon) roll against the creatures whose space they pass through's [Deflect](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Deflect) defense to deal the wielded weapon damage.
4. **Divine Blade** - When making a Diving Flurry add an additional 2d6 damage.

###### Ghost Warrior Armor

_Legacy, Armor_

The base item is a suit of armor.

1. **Incorporeal Dodge** - As a [Response](app://obsidian.md/Response) the wearer and all their equipment becomes intangible and amorphous until the beginning of their next turn, making them immune to non-magical attacks, but limiting their ability to interact with the world. This [Feature](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Features) cannot be used again until after a [Long Rest](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Long%20Rest).
2. **Not There** - As a [Response](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Response) the wearer blinks out of existence turn and blinks back into existence at the beginning of their next turn. This [Feature](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Features) cannot be used again until after a [Long Rest](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Long%20Rest).
3. **Ghostwalk** - As an [Action](app://obsidian.md/Action) the wearer and all their equipment becomes intangible and amorphous for 10 minutes making them immune to non-magical attacks. As an [Interaction](app://obsidian.md/Interaction) the wearer can make a [Moderate](app://obsidian.md/Moderate) [Will](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Will)+[Focus](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Focus) roll to interact with the physical world that [Round](app://obsidian.md/Round). The wearer can end the Ghostwalk with an [Interaction](app://obsidian.md/Interaction). This [Feature](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Features) cannot be used again until after a [Long Rest](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Long%20Rest).
4. **Not There** - As a [Response](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Response) the wearer can cast [Blink](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Blink) as a Tier 2 caster. The [Feature](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Features) cannot be used again until after a [Long Rest](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Long%20Rest).

###### Gloves of Missile Mastery

_Legacy, Item_

Base item is a fine pair of gloves.

1. **Fingertip Storage** - The gloves can store 40 pieces of ammunition and any amount of chosen ammunition can be recalled with a [Interaction](app://obsidian.md/Interaction).
2. **Catch Arrows** - As a [Response](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Response) the wearer can roll [Dexterity](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Dexterity)+[Focus](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Focus) to snatch ammunition fired at them. If that roll beats the attacker’s [Deflect](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Deflect) they can chose to fire the ammunition back dealing the same damage they would have taken if the ammunition had hit.
3. **Catch Missiles** - As a [Response](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Response) the wearer can roll [Dexterity](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Dexterity)+[Focus](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Focus) to snatch all ranged weapons fired at them regardless of size fired at them or a creature adjacent to them. If that roll beats the attacker’s [Deflect](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Deflect) they can chose to fire the ammunition back dealing the same damage they would have taken if the ammunition had hit.
4. **Catch Magic** - As a [Response](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Response) the wearer can roll [Dexterity](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Dexterity)+[Focus](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Focus) to snatch a spell targeting their [Deflect](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Deflect) defense. If that roll beats the attacker’s [Deflect](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Deflect) they can chose to fire the spell back dealing the same damage and having the same effect they would have experienced if the spell had hit.

###### Thunderer

_Legacy, Weapon_

The base item is a weapon.

2. **Thunderbolt** - The weapon gains the [Thrown](app://obsidian.md/Thrown) 12 [Tag](app://obsidian.md/Tags) and returns to the wielder’s hand at the end of their turn.
3. **Thunder Strike** - As an [Action](app://obsidian.md/Action) the wielder can deal 3d8 damage to all foes within a 6 space [Aura](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Aura) by rolling their [Will](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Will)+[Focus](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Focus) against the target’s [Body](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Body). As a [Perk](app://obsidian.md/Perk) the targets are [Deafened](app://obsidian.md/Deafened) until the end of their next turn.
4. **Arc Lighting** - As an [Action](app://obsidian.md/Action) the wielder can deal 6d8 damage to all foes in a 12 space [Line](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Line) by rolling their [Will](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Will)+[Focus](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Focus) against the target’s [Body](app://obsidian.md/index.html#Body). As a [Perk](app://obsidian.md/Perk) deal an additional 3d8 damage.
