What you reward will push the Players behavior. If you want to focus on killing monsters, Treasure will encourage that behavior. Advancement after  completing adventures or plots will drive engagement in solving them. Downtime and Renown pushed the players to invent in their Characters' personal development. The balance of rewards the Game Master provides will  affect the tenor of the game and direction the Characters pursue.

# Treasure
The simplest reward is material wealth, either in Coin, Materials, and Enchanted Items.

## Coin

### Bounties
Many malicious creatures will have bounties put on them by authorities to protect their people. The amount of the bounty should be based on the Tier of the creature and their history of preying on people. 

### Carried Coin
Creatures tend to carry an incidental amount of coin that meets their needs and supports their lifestyle. 

### Lairs

Lairs contain the wealth of the creature, finding a lair should provide a multiplier of the Carried Coin what coin is present.

## Materials

The more advanced or rare the creature the more potent their remains can be when used as Enhanced Component, be used in crafting Alchemical Items or Enchanted Items, or be sold or traded. 

## Alchemical Items
Alchemical Items grant one off features that provide a Character with a new Move. Because of their single use nature that can be given more freely than other Rewards. 

## Enchanted Items

Enchanted Items grant features that advance the Character's capabilities without increasing their level or Tier. 

### Identifying an Enchanted Item

Enchanted Items have a preternatural shine to them that distinguishes them from mundane item. The investigator rolls [[Glossary#Aptitude]]+[[Glossary#Craft]] plus the Arcana [[Glossary#Expertise]] against a [[Glossary#Target Number]] to identify the item and its [[Glossary#Features|Feature]].

If a creature attunes to an item before identifying its [[Glossary#Features]], they can make a [[Glossary#Aptitude]]+[[Glossary#Focus]] toll against the [[Glossary#Target Number]] to learn its [[Glossary#Features]].

| Tier   | [[Glossary#Target Number]] |
| :----- | :---------------- |
| Tier 1 | [[Glossary#Easy]]          |
| Tier 2 | [[Glossary#Moderate]]      |
| Tier 3 | [[Glossary#Hard]]          |
| Tier 4 | [[Glossary#Improbable]]    |

For consumable items the investigator can use the Alchemy Expertise and an Apothecary Pouch gives an Edge on their identification roll.


### Identifying an Artifact
Determining their [[Glossary#Features]] required knowledge of what artifact you are identifying or making a Hard [[Glossary#Aptitude]] + [[Glossary#Knowledge]] roll with any [[Glossary#History]] [[Glossary#Expetise|Expertises]] to identify the artifact and learn its history. As a [[Glossary#Perk]] the investigator also learns how to activate one [[Glossary#Features|Feature]]. As a [[Glossary#Complication]] the artifact is misidentified and all subsequent rolls to investigate its aspects have a [[Glossary#Hindrance]] until a successful investigation happens.  

A creature that can see magic can spend a Downtime to make an [[Glossary#Improbable]] [[Glossary#Aptitude]] + [[Glossary#Knowledge]] roll with the Arcana expertise to determine one [[Glossary#Features|Feature]] of the artifact. As a [[Glossary#Perk]] the investigator also learns how to activate the [[Glossary#Feature]]. As a [[Glossary#Complication]] one aspect is misidentified and its activation deals damage or causes a [[Glossary#Complication]] every time it is activated. This continues until the investigator spends a week and makes a successful [[Glossary#Improbable]] [[Glossary#Aptitude]] + [[Glossary#Knowledge]] roll with any applicable History [[Glossary#Expertise]].

# Advancement
Advancement it the most powerful form of reward. Each level increases the capability of the Character and each Tier increases the overall power level of the Character. Advancing Levels should be reserved for completing a story and advancing Tiers should be reserved for completing a full arc, leading to a more involved and expansive arc.

# Downtime
Every character can make minor advancements through multiple downtime actions. Giving out Downtime can increase the Player's investment in their Character.

# Renown
Renown provides Expertise that can be used out of combat or otherwise provide features they can use when interacting with NPCs.
