## Adherent
### Core Features
#### Tier 1
##### Divine Conduit
Adherents channel spells from a [[Divine]] source. Their [[Spellcasting Bonus]] is [[Will]]+[[Enigmas]], they use a Holy Symbol as a [[Foci]], and do not need a free hand to cast spells. They have access to a number of spells as their [[Aptitude]]+[[Knowledge]] to a minimum of one and two [[Cantrip|Cantrips]]. As a Downtime Action they can pray to be granted a different selection of Tier 1 spells. They have a base [[Burn]] of 6 that resets after a [[Long Rest]].

##### Domain
Adherents also have specific spells granted to them by their divinity. Every divinity has one or more domains that they have dominion over. Adherents channel their power through one of their divinity's Domains. The Adherent always has the Domain spell prepared and they don't count against the Adherent's list of prepared spells.

#### Tier 2

##### Divine Conduit
Adherents channel spells from a [[Divine]] source. Their [[Spellcasting Bonus]] is [[Will]]+[[Enigmas]], they use a holy symbol as a [[Foci]], and do not need a free hand to cast spells.  They have access to a number of spells their [[Aptitude]]+[[Knowledge]] to a minimum of one and three [[Cantrip|Cantrips]]. As a Downtime Action they can pray to be granted a different selection of Tier 1 and 2 spells. They have a base [[Burn]] of 4 that resets after a Long Rest.

##### Domain Blessing
Adherents gain [[Features]] granted to them by their deity based on their Domain.

###### Arcana 
**Spellbook** The Adherents of the arcana domain gain a spellbook that they can scribe [[Wizard]] spells into and prepare one of them for every Tier after a [[Long Rest]]. They can cast spells from their spellbook as a [[Ritual]].

###### Life
**Font of Life** When the Adherents of the Life domain casts [[Cure Wounds]] its casting time is an  [[Action]].

###### Order
**Call to Aid** As an [[Response]] the Adherents of the Order Domain can command an ally to move up to half their movement and make a single commanded [[Action]].

###### Sun
**Glorious Dawn** As a [[Maneuver]] a Adherents glows with a 4 spaces aura of [[Daylight]] for 1 minute. They have [[Edge]] on all [[Social]] rolls against creatures that can see them.

###### Tempest
**Call the Thunder** On an attack, if the Adherents's attack roll that hits exceeds their [[Burn]] they can increase their [[Burn]] by 1 to add 2d8 damage.

###### War
**Blessed Arms** As a maneuver the Adherent can designate and ally who's next attack will bypass [[Soak]].

#### Tier 3

##### Divine Conduit
Adherents channel spells from a Divine source. Their [[Spellcasting Roll]] is [[Will]]+[[Enigmas]], use a holy symbol as a [[Foci]], and do not need a free hand to cast spells. They have access to a number of spells their [[Aptitude]]+[[Knowledge]] to a minimum of one and four [[Cantrip|Cantrips]]. As a Downtime Action they can pray to be granted a different selection of 1, 2, and 3 spells. They have a base [[Burn]] of 2 that resets after a Long Rest.

##### Domain Boon

Adherents gain [[Features]] granted to them by their deity based on their Domain.

###### Arcana
**Countershock** As a [[Response]] to a target within 12 spaces casting a spell, the Adherents can deal 2d8 damage to a target within 6 spaces that is casting a spell. If the damage is greater than the [[Target Number]] the spell or the [[Spellcasting Roll]], the spell does not complete.

###### Life
**Channel of Life** When a Adherents casts a spell that heals [[HP]] or grants [[Temporary HP]], they gain that amount as well.

###### Order
**Divine Dictate** When the Adherents casts spells that make a target [[Charmed]] their [[Spellcasting Roll]] has an [[Edge]].

###### Sun
**Channel of the Sun** The Adherents gains [[Expertise]] on all rolls while within [[Daylight]].

###### Tempest
**Throw the Lightning** As a [[Maneuver]] the Tempest domain Adherents can cast Lighting Bolt as a Tier 1 spell, increasing [[Burn]] by 1.

###### War
**Battle Bond** As a [[Response]] to an adjacent ally being struck, the Adherents can switch places with the ally making the attack miss, and make a melee weapon attack against any creature within range.

#### Tier 4
##### Divine Conduit
Adherents channel spells from a Divine source. Their [[Spellcasting Roll]] is [[Will]]+[[Enigmas]], use a holy symbol as a [[Foci]], and do not need a free hand to cast spells. They have access to a number of spells their [[Aptitude]]+[[Knowledge]] to a minimum of one and five [[Cantrip|Cantrips]]. As a Downtime Action they can pray to be granted a different selection of Tier 1, 2, 3, and 4 spells. They have a base [[Burn]] of 0 that resets after a Long Rest.

##### Domain Benediction

Adherents gain [[Features]] granted to them by their deity based on their Domain.

###### Arcana
**Spell Mastery** Choose one 1st level [[Wizard]] spell, the Adherents can cast it as a Cantrip at its lowest level.

###### Life
**Master of Life** When a Adherents casts a spell that heals [[HP]] or grants [[Temporary HP]],  grant the maximum that the die would allow.

###### Order
**Divine Intercession** Once before a long rest, a Adherents can summon a Tier 3 Outsider (Celestial) that will do their bidding for 1 minute.

###### Sun
**Blinding Blows** When a Adherents hits with a melee weapon, if their roll exceeds their burn, the target is [[Blinded]] for a round.

###### Tempest
**Storm Aura** As an [[Action]] a Adherents can summon an 3 spaces aura. Foes that begin their [[Turn]] within the or move into it aura take 1d6 damage. As a [[Maneuver]] a Adherents can push a foe out of the aura. Ranged attacks and spells that target creatures within the aura have a [[Hindrance]].

###### War
**Command Liturgy** As a [[Response]] a Adherents can command an ally to move up to half of their movement and make an [[Action]].

### Inquisitor
#### Tier 1
##### Divine Smite
As an [[Interaction]] the Inquisitor makes a [[Spellcasting Roll]] against their [[Burn]] to deal an additional 2d8 damage to a target whose Deflect Defense they have overcome this turn. 
##### Divine Rebuke
As a [[Response]] to a for attacking the Inquisitor or an adjacent ally, they deal 1d8 damage to the foe and increases the defender's Defense roll by the same amount.
##### Holy Weapon
During a long rest the Inquisitor can designate a weapon that acts as a Foci. If used to cast a spell against a target's Deflect Defense, as long as the target is threatened by the caster, it deals weapon damage along with the spell effects.
#### Tier 2
Choose 2 Features.
##### Branding Smite
As an [[Interaction]] the Inquisitor makes a [[Spellcasting Roll]] against their [[Burn]] to deal an additional 2d8 damage to a target whose Deflect Defense they have overcome this turn. The target glows with an Aura 2 of [[Bright Light]] until the end of their next Turn. While they glow they cannot hide, cannot be invisible, and have a [[Hindrance]] on their [[Deflect]] Defense. Increase Burn by 1.
##### Bolstering Blow
As a [[Response]] to overcoming a foe's Deflect Defense, the Inquisitor makes a [[Spellcasting Roll]] against their [[Burn]] and allies in Aura 3 gain 2d8 [[Temporary HP]]. Increase Burn by 1.
##### Fearsome Smite
As an [[Interaction]] the Inquisitor makes a [[Spellcasting Roll]] against their [[Burn]] to deal an additional 2d8 damage to a target whose Deflect Defense they have overcome against this turn and the target is [[Frightened]] until end of their next Turn. Increase Burn by 1.
##### Slamming Smite
As an [[Interaction]] the Inquisitor makes a [[Spellcasting Roll]] against their [[Burn]] to deal an additional 2d8 damage to a target whose Deflect Defense they have overcome against this turn and the target is [[Prone]]. Increase Burn by 1.
#### Tier 3
Choose 2 Tier 2 or 3 Features.
##### Enervating Smite
As an [[Interaction]] the Inquisitormakes a [[Spellcasting Roll]] against their [[Burn]] to deal an additional 3d8 damage to a target whose Deflect Defense they have overcome this turn and the target is [[Blighted]]. Increase Burn by 2.
##### Extra Attack
As an [[Action]] a Inquisitor can make a weapon attack twice.
##### Thundering Smite
As an [[Interaction]] the Inquisitor makes a [[Spellcasting Roll]] against their [[Burn]] to deal an additional 3d8 damage to a target whose Deflect Defense they have overcome this turn and the target is pushed 3 spaces and [[Deafened]] until the end of their next Turn. Increase Burn by 2.
##### Vampiric Smite
As an [[Interaction]] the Inquisitor makes a [[Spellcasting Roll]] against their [[Burn]] to deal an additional 3d8 damage to a target whose Deflect Defense they have overcome this turn and gains that many [[HP]]. Increase Burn by 2.
#### Tier 4
Choose 2 Tier 3 or 4 Features.
##### Blinding Smite
As an [[Interaction]] the Inquisitor makes a [[Spellcasting Roll]] against their [[Burn]] to deal an additional 3d8 damage to a target whose Deflect Defense they have overcome this turn and the target is [[Blinded]] until the end of their next Turn. Increase Burn by 3.

If the target is an outsider the Seeker makes a [[Spellcasting Roll]] against the target's [[Mind]] Defense. If the roll succeeds, the Outsider does not return at the end of their next Turn. Increase Burn by 3.
##### Healing Blow
As a [[Response]] to overcoming a foe's Deflect Defense the Inquisitor makes a [[Spellcasting Roll]] against their [[Burn]] and allies in Aura 3 gain 2d8 [[HP]].  Increase Burn by 3.

## Artificer
Artificers merge mechanical, chemical, and magical arts to create mechanisms. Their favored stats are Aptitude and Wits. They are proficient in Craft, Knowledge, Weapons, and one additional Skill. Artificers have a [[Base HP]] of 6. They begin with a multitool, a codex, a Tinker's Parts kit, and a weapon. 
### Core Features
#### Tier 1
##### Multitool
The Artificer has a multitool that they built, it acts as Tinkerer's Tools. During a Short or Long Rest they can adjust it to contain one cantrip from their Codex. The [[Spellcasting Roll]] of spells cast through the Multitool is [[Wits]]+[[Weapons]]. Rolling a 1 on the Spellcasting Roll disables the Multitool until repaired during a Short Rest.
##### Manual
The Artificer uses a Codex to contain their formula and blueprints for their constructions. They start with two Cantrips and as many Tier 1 spells as their Aptitude. They can scribe a new spell or mechanism with a number of Downtime actions based on the Tier of the spell. An Artificer can only transcribe a spell of the Artificer's current Tier or lower. 
##### Empower Charge
During a Long Rest the Artificer can craft 2 + [[Aptitude]] ammunition worth of Tiered spells. These spells can be channeled through the Artificer's multitool to cast. These charges fade when the Artificer takes a Long Rest. When casting a spell as ammunition, as a Perk the ammunition is not expended.
#### Tier 2
Gain Expanded Multitool and one additional Feature
##### Expanded Multitool
During a Short or Long rest the Artificer  can adjust it to contain two cantrips from their Codex. Rolling a 1 on the Spellcasting Roll twice disables the Multitool until repaired during a Short Rest.
##### Eureka
When the Artificer gains Eureka they add an additional spell to their Manual of Tier 2 or lower and a Cantrip. 
##### Empower Ammunition
During a Long Rest the Artificer can craft 4 + [[Aptitude]] ammunition worth of Tiered spells. These spells can be channeled through the Artificer's multitool to cast. These charges fade when the Artificer takes a Long Rest. When casting a spell from a Charge, as a Perk the charge is not expended.
#### Tier 3
Gain Expanded Multitool and one additional Feature
##### Expanded Multitool
During a Short or Long rest the Artificer can adjust it to contain two cantrips from their Codex. Rolling a 1 on the Spellcasting Roll three times disables the Multitool until repaired during a Short Rest.
##### Eureka
When the Artificer gains Eureka they add an additional spell to their Manual of Tier 3 or lower and a Cantrip. 
##### Empower Charge
During a Long Rest the Artificer can craft 6 + [[Aptitude]] ammunition worth of Tiered spells. These spells can be channeled through the Artificer's multitool to cast. These charges fade when the Artificer takes a Long Rest. When casting a spell from a Charge, as a Perk the charge is not expended.
#### Tier 4
Gain Expanded Multitool and one additional Feature
##### Expanded Multitool
During a Short or Long rest the Artificer  can adjust it to contain two cantrips from their Codex. Rolling a 1 on the Spellcasting Roll four times disables the Multitool until repaired during a Short Rest.
##### Eureka
When the Artificer gains Eureka they add an additional spell to their Manual of Tier 4 or lower and a Cantrip. 
##### Empower Charge
During a Long Rest the Artificer can craft 8 + [[Aptitude]] ammunition worth of Tiered spells. These spells can be channeled through the Artificer's multitool to cast. These charges fade when the Artificer takes a Long Rest. When casting a spell from a Charge, as a Perk the charge is not expended.

As an [[Interaction]] the warmage gains 1d4 die of Temporary HP for every Tier of the previously cast spell. Increase Burn by 2
##### Surge
When the War Mage successfully counters a spell they gain a surge. They can spend surges equal to the Tier of a spell to cast it at one Tier higher. Their Surges reset during a [[Short Rest]] or [[Long Rest]].
##### Reflective Counter
When the War Mage counters a spell it targets its caster and uses the War Mage's Counterspell's [[Spellcasting Roll]] to overcome the target's defenses.
#### Tier 4
Choose 2 Tier 3 or 4 Features.
##### Arcane Defense
The War Mage has an edge on Defenses against spells.
##### Overcharged Conduit
The War Mage can cast multiple non-Cantrip spells in one Turn.
##### Quick Cast
As a [[Maneuver]] the War Mage can cast an Engraved Spell that has an [[Action]] Casting Time.

# Feats
## Tier 1
##### Arcane Mind
Gain the Arcane Expertise. If you already have the Arcane Expertise gain another Expertise. Arcane Expertise applies to Spellcasting Rolls and the spells you can prepare.
##### Battle Caster
You do not have a Hindrance on ranged spells cast while threatened. You have Edge on Spellcasting rolls against effects that will cancel your spell.
##### Brawler
You gain Expertise in Unarmed Strikes and Improvised Weapons. Your Unarmed Strike damage becomes a d4+Strength. As a Perk you can Grab, Blight, or knock the target Prone.
##### Charger
When you use your action to Dash, as a Maneuver you can make a melee weapon attack and knock the target Prone or push 3 spaces.
##### Clear Ally
As a [[Response]] to an ally failing a React or Deflect Defense, Shift them [[Strength]] spaces. If they are outside of the area that caused the Defense, they are not affected.
##### Crossbow Mastery
Weapons with the Mechanical Tag can be reloaded as an Interaction. Being threatened by an enemy does not give you a hindrance when using a weapon with the Ranged tag.
##### Crushing Grip
When you successfully grab a foe deal 1d6+Strength damage. Deal 1d6+Strength to the [[Grabbed]] target at the beginning of your Turn.
##### Diffusing Presence
As an Action you can roll Will+Social against the Mind Defense of foes who can see, hear, and understand. If you overcome their Defense, they will not act hostilely to you or your allies until one of your allies does. If they are Frightened they have a Hindrance on their Defense.
##### Disciple
Gain proficiency in the Divine Enigmas skill, learn two Cantrips from the Adherent's Spell list, and an Adherent’s Domain feature as appropriate for your Tier and the deity you are a disciple of.
##### Duelist
Weapons with the Finesse tag gain the Disarming Tag. As a Perk the target is Exposed and has a Hindrance on Deflect and React until the end of their next turn.
##### Dual Wielder
As a Maneuver you can make a weapon attack with a Finesse weapon. As a Reaction to a failed Deflect defense you can roll Dexterity+Weapons and use the higher Roll.
##### Evade
As a Reaction to a failed React or Deflect Defense, Roll Dexterity+Athletics and take the higher Roll.
##### Fearsome Spells
If a Spellcasting Roll exceeds the targets’ Mind defense the target is Frightened until the end of their Next turn.
##### Feinting Duelist
As a Maneuver, if you have an offhand weapon you can roll Wits+Weapon against the target’s Mind Defense. On a successful roll, the target is Exposed. As a Perk, deal the Weapon’s damage.
##### Flashing Spell
As a Perk for Spellcasting Rolls, the target is Blinded until the end of their next Turn and does not increase Burn.
##### Gain Expertise
You gain an Expertise.
##### Generous Ally
As a Maneuver you can Assist an ally.
##### Grappler
You can use any weapon without the Ranged, Two-Handed, or Versatile tag against a grabbed target.
##### Green Soul
Gain the Nature Expertise. If you already have the Nature Expertise gain another Expertise. Nature Expertise applies to Spellcasting Rolls and the spells you can prepare.
##### Ground Warrior
You gain no penalties to standing and can move your full stride speed while Prone.
##### Illuminating Spell
Target of a successful spell glows with 6 spaces of Daylight until the end of their next Turn. Areas of a spell glow until the end of the turn, or the end of the spell, whichever is longer.
##### Improve Stat
Improve Strength, Fortitude, Dexterity, Wits, Aptitude, or Will by 1.
##### Lightfooted
You move through Difficult Terrain without a penalty to speed, have a Climb speed equal to half your Stride, and are considered one size smaller to trigger traps.
##### Linguist
You gain your Aptitude bonus in Languages.
##### Lunger
Your melee weapon attacks gain Reach 2. You cannot reach through an occupied space.
##### Mobile
Enemies cannot make Movement Responses against you.
##### Mounted Combat
You have Edge on attacks against enemies smaller than your mount. Attacks that target your mount target you instead. As a Reaction to an area effect, you can spur your mount to move half their Stride, if the mount ends their move outside the area neither you or the mount are affected.
##### Offbalance Exploit
As a Reaction to a failed Melee attack against you from a creature the same size or smaller, switch places with the attacker. The attacker is Exposed.
##### Overwhelming Threat 
As an Action you can make a Melee Weapon Attack against a Foe. All Foes within line of sight of you whose Mind Defense is overcome by your attack roll have a Hindrance on rolling against anyone but you.
##### Parkour
Increase Stride by 2 spaces. Gain Climb speed equal to Stride speed. Difficult and Hazardous terrain does not reduce your speed.
##### Parry
As a Reaction to a melee or touch attack against your Deflect Defense, roll Dexterity+Weapon against the foe’s attack. On a success the attack is negated and the foe is Exposed.
##### Penitent Soul
Gain the Religion Expertise. If you already have the Religion Expertise gain another Expertise. Apply Religion Expertise to Spellcasting Rolls and the spells you can prepare.
##### Poisoner
As an action you can apply poison to a weapon. During a Short Rest create one charge of poison that deals 1d8 damage from a creature with a poison attack with a Moderate Aptitude+Knowledge Roll. As a Downtime Action you can brew 6 poisons of higher potency with an Aptitude+Knowledge Roll with 2d8 damage with a Moderate Roll, 3d8 damage with a Hard Roll, and 4d8 damage with an Improbable Roll. As a Complication you are blighted for every die of damage the poison has. As a Perk the poisons Blight the target for each die of damage the poison has.
##### Recover Center
As an interaction you can re-roll a Mind defense against an ongoing effect.
##### Roll Clear
As a Reaction you can move half your Stride speed. If you move out of the area of an effect or beyond the reach of an attacking foe, you take no damage and are unaffected.
##### Shield Master
Items with the Shield tag deal 1d8 damage. As a Perk the foe is pushed two spaces and knocked prone.
##### Stumbling Spell
As a Perk for Spellcasting Rolls, the target is knocked Prone and does not increase Burn.
##### Throwing Arm
Add your Strength to the distance of weapons with the Thrown tag.
##### Weapon Adept
As an Action, make a Weapon Attack that applies the Perk instead of dealing damage. As a Perk the attack deals the weapon damage.
## Tier 2
##### Alertness
You gain Edge on React Rolls. If you do not go on the First Round of combat, you gain Edge on all React and Deflect rolls until the beginning of your next turn.
##### Allied Assistance
As a Reaction to an ally hitting a target with a Melee Weapon Attack, you can use that Roll to make an Melee Weapon Attack.
##### Archetype Adept
Choose two additional Tier 2 Archetype or Specialty Features.
##### Choking Grip
As a Maneuver, make a Strength+Athletics roll against a Grabbed Target’s Body. If the defense is overcome the target begins to suffocate. Dealing damage to the target reduces the rounds they can suffocate by 1.
##### Club Mastery
Weapons with the Slam tag push the Target two spaces when they hit. As a Perk they are knocked prone.
##### Coordinated
Maneuvers that don't include movement now can be preformed and move your Stride Speed.
##### Disarming Mastery
As an Action you can make a Melee Weapon Attack, if the Roll exceeds the target’s Body take one of their held items into a free hand. If you do not have a free hand, the item lands in a space of your choice within 3 spaces.
##### Distracting Duelist
As an Action you can make a Melee Weapon Attack, if the Roll exceeds the target’s mind the target is exposed. As a Perk allies gain an Edge on rolls against the target.
##### Dying Fury
Dropping below 0 hit points does not make you unconscious and weapon attacks have Edge.
##### Exploiting Focus
You have an Edge on Rolls against Exposed foes.
##### Gain Skill
You gain a new Skill.
##### Guarded
You have an edge in Rolls with of a chosen Defense.
##### Guardian
As a Reaction to a Foe moving through a threatened space make a melee weapon attack. If the attack hits the target loses any remaining movement until the end of their Turn.
##### Flanking Adept
When foe is exposed from Flanking, you have an Edge on Rolls against that target.
##### Frightening Blow
With an Action you can make a Weapon Attack, if the Roll also defeats the target’s Mind Defense, the target is Frightened until the end of their next turn.
##### Inspire Allies
As an Action you grant allies that can hear you Edge on their next Attack Roll.
##### Lock Down Strike
As an Action you can make a weapon attack, if it succeeds the target cannot take a Maneuver to move. As a Perk, the target has a hindrance on Attacks until the end of their next turn.
##### Mage Slayer
If you deal damage with a Reaction, the target has a Hindrance on spellcasting.
##### Morale Breaker
If you reduce a target to 0 HP all foes within 6 spaces of you are Frightened until the end of their next Turn.
##### Rally the Fallen
As a Reaction to an ally failing a Stabilization Roll that can hear you, they can reroll the save and take the higher result.
##### Reach Spell
Cantrips with Target of Touch gain 6 space Range. Spells with Target of Touch cast as a Tier higher gain 6 space Range without gaining the higher tier cast bonus. Spells with a Range cast as a Tier higher and double the spaces without gaining the higher tier cast bonus.
##### Reposte
As a Reaction to a Melee attack against you, roll Dexterity+Weapon against the foe’s attack. On a success the attack is negated, the foe is Exposed, and they take damage from the weapon.
##### Rideby Attack
Your mount does not provoke Movement Responses.
##### Shadow Cloak
While in low light or darkness you are invisible.
##### Shape Spell
You can exclude targets within the area of a spell from the effects of the spell.
##### Shield Save
As a Reaction to a failed React or Deflect while wielding a shield you can roll your Strength+Weapons and take the higher roll.
##### Silent Spell
A Spell cast as a Tier higher without gaining the higher tier cast bonus don’t have to be spoken. Rolling Wits+Enigma against creatures’ React Defense prevents them from detecting the spell.
##### Sweeping Blow
As an Action you can make a single Melee Weapon Attack Roll against all targets you threaten.
##### Trampling Charge
As an Action move your Stride Speed, make a Melee Weapon Attack against all foes who’s space you move through. If you are mounted, use your mount’s stride speed and a Melee Weapon Attack of theirs.
##### Tripping Blow
As an Action you can make a Melee Weapon Attack against a Foe, if it exceeds the target’s Deflect the target takes weapon damage. If it exceeds the target’s React, the target is knocked Prone.
##### Warmage
You gain Expertise in Spells that deal damage and have an Edge on Focus Rolls.
##### Weapon Master
When you hit with a weapon that you have Expertise in, apply the Perk effect. As a Perk, apply two Perks.
## Tier 3
##### Archetype Master
Choose two additional Tier 2 or 3 Archetype or Specialty Features.
##### Blademaster
Weapons with the Bladed Tag grant the Bleeding condition and as a Perk deal an additional die of damage.
##### Blind Fighting
You gain Blindsight 3 spaces.
##### Bloody Menace
If you make the target Bleed it also Blights them.
##### Blunt Blow
Weapons with the Blunt Tag Blight the target.
##### Dazzling Attack
When you deal damage to a Foe if the attack roll overcomes the Mind Defense of all foes within 6 spaces, those that fail are Frightened until the end of their next Turn.
##### Disabling Blow
As an Action you can make a Weapon Attack against a target and if it exceeds the target’s Body Defense one of the target’s Actions always fails until they regain HP.
##### Draining Grip
As a Maneuver you can drain 2d8 HP from a Grabbed target and gain that amount of Temporary HP.
##### Elemental Infusion
Your weapon deals an additional die of damage of an elemental type.
##### Embodied Focus
Your body is your Spellcasting Foci.
##### Followup Perk
When you gain a weapon Perk, make a second weapon attack in addition to the Perk’s effect.
##### Hardened Stat
You have an edge in Rolls with of a chosen Stat
##### Highly Reactive
You have two reactions per turn.
##### Knockout Grip
As an Action you can make a Strength+Athletics roll against a Grabbed target’s Body. On a failure they become Unconscious.
##### Misleading Perk
As a Perk the target cannot see you until the end of their next turn.
##### Never Say Die
Allies within 3 spaces have an Edge on Stabilization Rolls.
##### Presence of Doom
As a Maneuver you can roll Will+Social to make all targets within 6 spaces Frightened until the end of their next Turn.
##### Snap Cast
Cast a Cantrip that normally is an Action as a Maneuver.
##### Support Allies
As an Action you grant allies that can hear you Edge on their next Roll.
##### Thunder Stomp
As an Action you can roll Strength+Athletics against the target’s Body Defense to knock all creatures within an Aura 3 Prone.
## Tier 4
##### Armor of Shadows
While in Dim Light or Darkness your Soak becomes 10. As a Maneuver you can become invisible and move your Stride speed.
##### Blink Strike
When you overcome a target’s Defense, teleport to be adjacent to the target.
##### Frenetic Reactions
You have three reactions per turn.
##### Transcendent Archetype
Choose two additional Tier 3 or 4 Archetype or Specialty Features.
##### Uncovered Eyes
You gain Truesight to 12 spaces.
