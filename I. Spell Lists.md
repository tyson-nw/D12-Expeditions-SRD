- **Tier** Cantrip - Adherent
- **Casting Time** Action
- **Target** 12 space Range, Body
- **Duration** -

Target takes 1d6 damage. If they are already wounded the target takes 1d10 instead. As a [Perk](app://obsidian.md/Perk) they gain the [Bleeding](app://obsidian.md/Bleeding) condition. Increase the die for every Tier the caster is beyond 1st.
